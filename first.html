How the VISA Process Works:

Application Process - The traveler applies for a visa through an embassy, consulate, or an online portal (eVisa). - Required documents may include a passport, application form, photos, financial proof, and an invitation letter. - Some visas require an **interview** or **biometric data (fingerprints & photo)**. **Approval & Issuance** - Authorities review the application and conduct security checks. - If approved, the visa is stamped in the passport or issued electronically (eVisa). Arrival & Immigration Check** - Upon arrival, the traveler presents the visa and passport to immigration officers. -

Officers verify the visa, ask questions, and may grant entry.
